# Retail Sales Data Analysis (EDA Project)

## Objective
The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to identify patterns, trends, and insights that can help in better business decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- VS Code 

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains information about retail transactions, including:
- Date of purchase
- Product Category
- Quantity of items sold
- Total Amount of sales

## Steps Performed

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Checked for missing values
- Removed null values to ensure data quality

### 2. Descriptive Statistics
- Analyzed mean, minimum, maximum, and standard deviation of numerical columns

### 3. Data Analysis
- Performed category-wise sales analysis
- Identified most popular product categories
- Analyzed sales trends over time

### 4. Data Visualization
- Bar chart for product category distribution
- Pie chart for sales contribution by category
- Line chart for sales over time
- Heatmap to understand correlation between variables

##  Insights

- The Clothing category shows the highest number of transactions, making it the most popular category.
- Some product categories contribute less to overall sales, indicating lower demand.
- Sales vary over time, with certain dates showing higher sales than others.
- A strong relationship exists between quantity purchased and total sales amount.
- A few transactions generate significantly higher revenue compared to others.

##  Recommendations

- Increase stock availability for top-selling categories such as Clothing to maximize revenue.
- Provide discounts and promotional offers to boost sales of low-performing categories.
- Plan marketing strategies during high-demand periods to increase overall sales.
- Introduce combo deals and special offers to encourage customers to buy more products.
- Study customer purchasing patterns to enhance business decision-making.

## Conclusion
This project successfully analyzed retail sales data using EDA techniques. The insights gained can help businesses optimize inventory, improve marketing strategies, and enhance overall sales performance.
